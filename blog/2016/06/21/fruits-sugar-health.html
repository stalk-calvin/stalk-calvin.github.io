Sugar in fruits - Are they healthy?

Yesterday, during my lunch time at the company I work, colleague of mine said "Sugar is also in fruits" as if sugar in fruits are bad. So I went on and digged up the articles on what kind of effect it has on us.

Some facts about Sugar in Fruits

  • "fruit is all sugar"
    • I am not sure how this started in the first place. It is true that fruits contain sugar however they are no where close to comparing with table sugars, soda, junk foods etc. Scroll below to see the comparison of sugars in fruits vs junk foods.
    • Yes, fruits contain the 3 elements of sugar: fructose, glucose, and sucrose. However, they are natural source of sugar instead of added ones (empty calorie). Also fruits consist of "complex" carbohydrates where added sugars are simple ones.
    • Fruits also contain many other - including water, vitamins, minerals, fiber and phytonutrien etc. AND it is important to note they are very low in calories. I mean why would you eat anything else to replace fruits!?
  • Are fruits really healthy snack choice?
    • I would say yes, vegitables still would be a better choice if you do not need a good taste factor. However, it is a definitely a better choice than those human-made bars or those GMO foods.

Dried fruits, are they healthy or unhealthy?
